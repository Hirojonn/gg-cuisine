Top Filipino Cuisines

Adobong Manok

This is a recipe post for Filipino Pork Adobo. It is a dish composed of pork slices cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Sinigang Na Baboy

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient. Other proteins and seafood can also be used. Beef, shrimp, fish are commonly used to cook sinigang.

Spicy Bicol Express

The mild sweetness of coconut milk brings a sense of delight to the sharp notes of chili pepper and bagoong in the classic Bicol Express recipe.

Lechon

Lechon, derived from a Spanish word for roasted suckling pig is one of the most popular dishes in the Philippines.
